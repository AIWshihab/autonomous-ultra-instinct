# Glossary

Key terms used by the autonomous repair agent project.

- **Action**: A proposed remediation step generated by the planner.
- **Candidate action**: Any action generated for a detected issue before policy evaluation.
- **Decision trace**: A structured audit trail of detection, planning, policy, dispatch, execution, and verification steps.
- **Detection reason**: The explanation for why an issue was identified.
- **Issue**: A detected problem in the target environment.
- **Policy engine**: The component classifying actions by risk, confidence, and approval requirements.
- **Verification**: The process of confirming whether a simulated or executed action achieved its goal.
- **Risk tier**: A policy label such as low, medium, or high that reflects action impact.
- **Execution mode**: The intended handling of an action, e.g. `simulate_only`, `execute_when_safe`, or `blocked`.
- **Approval required**: Indicates whether a human reviewer is needed before action execution.
